True ↔︎ Compass, simply.

A compass rose adjusted to your boat's deviation table and your local variation.

Converting between True, magnetic and compass can get really confusing.

A curve is fitted to your deviations using a mathematical model.

This computed curve is then used to create a custom rose that has true on the outer ring and your compass on the inner ring. Variation is also applied making converting true into compass a simple lookup.
