Privacy Policy

We've tried to keep this privacy policy simple and clear.

Basics

  • We will not sell or distribute any personal information you share with us without your permission.
  • We will not send you unsolicited email, only email that relates to your use of this website.
  • We will gladly show you the personal data we store about you in order to run the website.

Email and mailing lists

We occasionally send email for purposes such as telling you your password when you create an account, when you've forgotten your password, or similar. We may also send you an email when a change occurs on the site that might affect your use of the site.

We operate an announcement mailing list using the MailChimp mailing list service. If you are receiving email from this list it is because you subscribed and then opted in. If you'd like to leave the list there is an unsubscribe link at the bottom of every email. You can also read MailChimp's policies.

Cookie policy

We use cookies to store that you've signed in to the site. We also use cookies to prevent cross site request forgery when you submit forms on this site. Without cookies it is not possible to use the parts of this site that require you to log in.

These are the cookies that we use:

Name Purpose Expires
sessionid Tracking who you are logged into the site as. 2 weeks
csrftoken Preventing cross site request forgery - which is where another site tries to make your browser do something on this site. This cookie just stores a random string that the other site cannot know. 1 year

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to see how users use our site. This allows us to improve the site based on how people use it.

Google Analytics sets the following cookies:

Name Purpose Expires
_ga Used to distinguish users. 2 years
_gat Used to throttle request rate. 10 minutes

For more details on Google Analytics cookies please see their guide.

Stripe payments

We charge for some of the services we provide and we use Stripe to collect these payments. We never see your credit card details nor do we store them on our servers.

Email sending through Postmark

We use an email sending service from Postmark for emails such as password reminders etc.
