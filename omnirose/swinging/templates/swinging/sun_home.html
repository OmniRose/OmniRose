Using the Sun to swing a compass

Assuming it's not cloudy, of course…

The Sun as a fixed reference point

By getting the sun to cast a shadow onto a compass rose and knowing where the sun is in the sky it is possible to precisely determine the direction your boat is pointing in.

The solar pelorus

A pelorus is a fancy name for something that tells you the angle to something from you boat. In our case it is the angle to the sun.

What makes ours special is that it is so easy to set up using things that are certain to be on your boat - drinks cans.

Equipment needed

  • A drinks can (not dented)
  • A print out of the solar pelorus
  • Something to mount the pelorus on
